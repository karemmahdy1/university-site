Classic Spaghetti Carbonara

Classic Spaghetti Carbonara is a comforting Italian dish made with simple, high-quality ingredients. This creamy pasta features crispy pancetta, rich egg yolks, and a generous amount of Parmesan cheese. It's a quick and satisfying meal that brings a taste of Italy to your table.

  1. Cook Pasta: Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil. Add 12 ounces of spaghetti and cook according to the package instructions until al dente. Reserve 1 cup of pasta water, then drain the pasta.
  2. Prepare Sauce: In a bowl, whisk together 3 large egg yolks, 1 whole egg, 1 cup of grated Parmesan cheese, and a pinch of black pepper. Set aside.
  3. Cook Pancetta: In a large skillet over medium heat, cook 8 ounces of diced pancetta until it's crispy and the fat is rendered, about 5-7 minutes.
  4. Combine Ingredients: Reduce the heat to low and add the cooked spaghetti to the skillet with the pancetta. Toss to combine.
  5. Add Sauce: Remove the skillet from the heat and quickly pour in the egg and cheese mixture, tossing the pasta continuously to create a creamy sauce. If the sauce is too thick, add some of the reserved pasta water a little at a time until you reach the desired consistency.
  6. Serve: Serve immediately, garnished with extra Parmesan cheese and freshly ground black pepper.

Spicy Shrimp Tacos

These Spicy Shrimp Tacos are bursting with flavor and perfect for a quick and delicious dinner. Juicy shrimp are seasoned with a blend of spices, quickly sautéed, and served in warm tortillas with a zesty slaw and creamy avocado sauce. It’s a fiesta in every bite!

  1. Prepare Shrimp: In a bowl, combine 1 pound of peeled and deveined shrimp with 1 teaspoon of chili powder, 1/2 teaspoon of smoked paprika, 1/2 teaspoon of cumin, 1/4 teaspoon of cayenne pepper, 1/4 teaspoon of garlic powder, and salt and pepper to taste. Toss to coat evenly.
  2. Make Slaw: In a separate bowl, mix together 2 cups of shredded cabbage, 1/4 cup of chopped cilantro, the juice of 1 lime, 1 tablespoon of olive oil, and a pinch of salt. Set aside.
  3. Prepare Avocado Sauce: In a blender or food processor, combine 1 ripe avocado, 1/4 cup of Greek yogurt, the juice of 1 lime, 1 small garlic clove, and a pinch of salt. Blend until smooth and creamy.
  4. Cook Shrimp: In a large skillet over medium-high heat, add 1 tablespoon of olive oil. Once hot, add the seasoned shrimp and cook for 2-3 minutes on each side, or until the shrimp are pink and opaque.
  5. Assemble Tacos: Warm your tortillas (8 small tortillas) in a dry skillet or microwave. To assemble, spread a spoonful of the avocado sauce on each tortilla, add a few shrimp, and top with the cabbage slaw.
  6. Serve: Serve the tacos with lime wedges and extra cilantro for garnish.

Vegetable Stir-Fry with Tofu

This Vegetable Stir-Fry with Tofu is a quick and nutritious dish packed with vibrant veggies and crispy tofu, all tossed in a savory soy-garlic sauce. Perfect for a healthy and satisfying weeknight dinner, this stir-fry is a feast for the eyes and the taste buds.

  1. Prepare Tofu: Press 1 block (14 oz) of extra-firm tofu to remove excess moisture. Cut the tofu into 1-inch cubes.
  2. Cook Tofu: In a large skillet or wok, heat 2 tablespoons of vegetable oil over medium-high heat. Add the tofu cubes and cook until golden and crispy on all sides, about 7-10 minutes. Remove from the skillet and set aside.
  3. Prepare Vegetables: While the tofu is cooking, chop 1 red bell pepper, 1 yellow bell pepper, 1 zucchini, 1 carrot, and 1 cup of broccoli florets. Mince 3 garlic cloves and 1-inch piece of ginger.
  4. Make Sauce: In a small bowl, whisk together 1/4 cup of soy sauce, 2 tablespoons of hoisin sauce, 1 tablespoon of rice vinegar, and 1 teaspoon of cornstarch.
  5. Cook Vegetables: In the same skillet, add another tablespoon of vegetable oil if needed. Add the garlic and ginger, and cook until fragrant, about 1 minute. Add the chopped vegetables and stir-fry for 5-7 minutes, or until tender-crisp.
  6. Combine and Serve: Return the tofu to the skillet and pour the sauce over the stir-fry. Toss to coat everything evenly and cook for another 2 minutes until the sauce thickens. Serve immediately, garnished with sesame seeds and chopped green onions if desired.
